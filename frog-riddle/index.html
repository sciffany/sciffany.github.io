Explanation:
We make a model such that at any point in time, there is a certain probability, 1/1, that male frogs croak.

Using Bayes' rule, we can calculate 2 probabilities:

(1) Given that you heard a croak from the left, what is the probability that the left-side croak comes from a male-female pair?

(2) Given that it was silent on the right side, what is the probability that the silence comes from a female?
