How to Play

The goal of Dicey Mice is to get the best dice scores possible for each category.

A game of dicey mice consists of 15 rounds. In each round you can throw the dice up to three times.

Let's start at the beginning with an example.
You roll the dice by clicking on Mildred the Mouse.
When Mildred spits the dice out, you click on the dice you wish to save. For example, let's assume you rolled the following:


You want to save the two dice showing five green dots.
To move the dice to the safe zone, click on them.
They will move to the highlighted area below.
If you change your mind, and want to move on back, click on the die in the safe zone.

Once you are finished selecting the dice you wish to keep, click on Mildred again to roll the remaining dice.




Another five! Click on it to place it in the safe zone and then click Mildred again to roll the remaining dice.

Let's assume you ended up with the following dice at the end of three rolls. You will notice in the score boxes below what your options are to save dice.



Remember! You can only use the same scorebox one time in each game. With this hand, your best option would be to select FOUR OF A KIND which will give you 51 points. (25 + total of dice).

On the top row, if you obtain a total of 63 points or more, you will receive 35 bonus points! This means you could also select 20 points in the FIVE box.

The game ends when you have selected every box once.
The CHANCE box allows you to save any combination of dice to maximize your points.

Scoring:
The top row scores points for having the same dice. For exmple, if you have three number TWO dice showing at the end of a round, you will be able to save SIX points in the TWO box.
