Instructions
Shuffle the letters to guess the right word. There is only one correct answer. Anagrams of the correct answer will not be accepted. Type in your answer using your keyboard or mouse.

Keyboard shortcuts
Spacebar - shuffle letters
Shift+backspace - clear word
Enter - reveal next letter
1 - previous word
2 - next word
